PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Our team undertook the comprehensive landscaping for Triveni Temple, creating a harmonious outdoor environment that complements the temple’s serene atmosphere. We installed interlocking pathways around the temple to provide a polished look and ease of access. Beautiful planters were crafted and filled with over 1,000 small plants, adding vibrant greenery throughout the site.

We also planted trees and completed sodding across the temple grounds to ensure a lush, green landscape. The highlight of this project is the central round waterfall, which serves as a tranquil focal point, enhancing the overall aesthetic of this sacred space.
